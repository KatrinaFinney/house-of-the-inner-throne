## The Inner Law

Attention directs energy.

Whatever repeatedly receives attention gradually expands within the practitioner's experience. Thoughts, goals, fears, and opportunities all grow stronger when sustained by attention.

For this reason, attention functions as a form of spiritual currency.

## A Mirror for the Practitioner

Many individuals believe external forces determine their circumstances entirely.

Yet a closer examination often reveals that attention has been invested repeatedly in the very patterns they wish to escape.

Where attention flows, influence follows.

## The Hidden Mechanism

The mind strengthens whatever it observes repeatedly.

When attention returns again and again to a belief or situation, the mind reinforces the pathways that sustain that experience.

By directing attention intentionally, the practitioner gradually reshapes perception and behavior.

## Where This Appears in Daily Life

Attention influences every environment.

The conversations one entertains, the problems one dwells upon, and the possibilities one imagines gradually define the emotional and intellectual climate of life.

Intentional attention changes this climate.

## The Economy of Awareness

Just as financial resources must be invested wisely, attention must be allocated carefully.

The practitioner who governs attention gradually gains influence over the direction of their life.

This discipline becomes a form of spiritual wealth.

## The Language of Color

Green represents prosperity, growth, and balanced expansion.

Lighting a **green candle** reinforces focus on constructive development and opportunity.

## Returning to the Inner Throne

Attention determines what grows.

The practitioner who governs attention gradually governs their environment.

## The Rite of Alignment

### The Intelligence Behind the Lesson

Lakshmi, the Hindu goddess of prosperity and harmony, represents abundance that arises through clarity, gratitude, and balanced attention.

Her iconography frequently depicts flowing coins and lotus blossoms, symbols of prosperity that emerges when energy circulates freely and attention remains aligned with growth.

Within the Inner Throne system, Lakshmi represents the intelligence of focused prosperity.

### Energetic Current

The focused prosperity current strengthens:

• gratitude for existing resources  
• clarity of mental focus  
• attraction of constructive opportunities  
• balanced expansion rather than chaotic growth

When attention becomes disciplined, prosperity becomes easier to recognize and cultivate.

### Communion Ritual

Place a **green candle** before you and light it slowly.

Sit comfortably and take several slow breaths.

Reflect on where your attention has been invested recently. Consider both the thoughts that strengthen your life and those that drain your energy.

Write down **three areas where your attention deserves greater investment**.

Speak softly:

“My attention nourishes what is worthy of growth.”

Spend several minutes observing the candle flame while imagining your attention flowing toward the areas you have chosen.

### The Lunar Gate

The **Waxing Gibbous Moon** represents refinement and preparation before completion. During this phase, attention becomes sharper and plans become clearer.

Rituals performed during this phase strengthen strategic focus and prosperity development.
