# Volume IV  
# The Lineage of Spirit

This volume turns toward ancestry, spiritual communication, boundaries, and the subtle intelligence of the unseen world.

Its teachings concern remembrance, libation, restoration, names, protection, spirit allies, atmosphere, and the final return to sovereignty. Here the practitioner is asked to mature in discernment: to recognize that spiritual relationship is sustained not by spectacle, but by patience, respect, and disciplined listening.

Volume IV teaches the reader to stand within lineage without surrendering autonomy.

The practitioner remembers the dead, repairs the self, calls names with intention, protects their space, and listens for the quiet movement of spirit. In doing so, they discover that the unseen world is not separate from ordinary life. It is woven through it.

These final manuscripts close the canon by returning the reader to responsibility.

Enter this volume with reverence.

Its lessons do not remove the burden of discernment. They refine it.
