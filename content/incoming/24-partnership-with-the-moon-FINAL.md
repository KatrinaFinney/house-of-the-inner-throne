## The Inner Law

Nature moves in cycles.

The moon provides one of the clearest reminders of this rhythm.

## A Mirror for the Practitioner

Even without formal training, most people sense the calm of a full moon or the quiet reflection of the dark moon.

## The Hidden Mechanism

The lunar cycle provides a visible calendar for intention.

## Where This Appears in Daily Life

Farmers, sailors, and many traditional cultures have long observed lunar rhythms to guide planting, travel, and ritual timing.

## The Patience of Cycles

Working with the moon encourages patience. Intentions planted during early phases gradually mature as the moon grows.

## The Language of Color

Silver represents lunar reflection and intuitive awareness.

## Returning to the Inner Throne

The moon teaches patience and respect for natural timing.
